Cable Beach

Named in honour of the Java-to-Australia undersea telegraph cable which reaches shore there, Cable Beach is situated 7km (4.3 miles) from town along a bitumen road

What Visitors Say

A sunset that everyone should see and a chance to dip your toes in the Indian Ocean. I visited this beach nightly to watch the sun set, each one different but each one spectacular.

Trip Advisor

Western Australia has some of the most beautiful beaches in the world and this is definitely one of them.
